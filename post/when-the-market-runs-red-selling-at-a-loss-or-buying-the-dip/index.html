For those brave enough to buy the dip in NVIDIA, remember: you’re not just buying stocks—you’re investing in the future of computational power.

VI. 2025 U.S. Stock Survival Guide: Picking Up Gold Coins Amidst the Carnage

  • The Double-Edged Sword of Leverage: Data from Goldman Sachs reveals a clear deleverage-rebound cycle.
  • The New Take on ‘Cash is King’: It’s not about passively holding cash, but arming yourself with “bullets” while waiting for the target.

Remember: In a turbulent market, managing your positions trumps stock picking.

6.2 The Alchemy of Individual Stocks

Navigating individual stock picks can be an exercise in both art and risk:

  • The Quantum Scam Stock Lesson: In a Gen Z-dominated market, the story often outweighs the fundamentals.

Final advice: Thinking of trading individual stocks? Read “The Self-Cultivation of Retail Investors” at least three times before diving in!
